Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)

calories/day
BMR is the number of calories your body burns at rest to maintain basic functions like breathing and circulation.

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE)

calories/day
TDEE is your total daily calorie needs, including physical activity. This is your maintenance calorie level.
